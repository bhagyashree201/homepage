ABOUT US

Join as a College Representative for Megalith, IIT Kharagpur and be a part of the new and varied experiences that follow! Grab a unique opportunity to enhance your skills in the fields of publicity, marketing, networking, and maintaining public relations at a national level by acting as the College Representative for your college.



RESPONSIBILITIES

A CR will be called successful CR if he/she is able to bring at least 10 participants at the time of fest.




Leadership

Create awareness and buzz about Megalith, IIT KGP by acting as the Point of Contact between us and your college.

Publicity

Informing the students in your institute about our events and activities through various means, including social media

Participation

Participants are the lifelines for any fest. Ensure maximum participation from your college in workshops and competitions.



INCENTIVES

What will you gain as a College Representative .




Certification

A Certificate of Appreciation will be awarded on successful completion of CRP(College Representative Program).

Communication Skills

Build your communication skills and gain exposure in events promotion, organization and brand management at a national level.

Workshops

Privilege to attend a workshop by experienced faculty to enhance your knowledge free of cost during fest.

Recognition

Recognition in the name of Megalith '22. The names of the best performing CRs will be put up on our FB page.



The Better You Work, The Better You Win

Incentives Based on your Performance -Top 3 CRs would recieve Coupons.
